# Communication Style

## Formatting
- Use bullet points, not long paragraphs
- Keep things scannable and concise
- No em dashes. Ever.

## Tone
- Casual or professional depending on context
- Same tone for internal and external/public content
- Direct. Don't over-explain.

## LinkedIn / Public Content Style
- Write in "I-language" (first person, reflective)
- Use short lines with deliberate line breaks for rhythm
- Structure: hook > story/insight > takeaway > bullet list > closing line
- Minimal emojis (1-2 max per post, not more)
- No em dashes
- Thoughtful, not salesy
- Reference: See Shaid's published LinkedIn posts for exact voice and cadence

## Instagram Content
- Nate Herk style video content (to be developed)
- Convert insights and ideas into short-form video format

## General
- Never add unnecessary emojis to files or messages
- Lead with the point, not the preamble
