# Content Creation Rules

## LinkedIn Posts
- Search for viral posts and trending topics as inspiration
- Match Shaid's existing LinkedIn voice (I-language, reflective, structured)
- Include an image concept with Shaid's photo when possible
- Post structure:
  1. Hook (attention-grabbing first line)
  2. Story or insight (personal angle)
  3. Key takeaway or framework
  4. Bullet list of actionable points
  5. Strong closing line
- Keep posts authentic. No corporate fluff.

## Instagram
- Convert viral YouTube videos or trending topics into short-form video scripts
- Nate Herk style: direct to camera, punchy, value-packed
- This workflow is being developed. Start simple.

## Content Workflow (to be templatized)
1. Find trending topic or viral content
2. Draft post in Shaid's voice
3. Suggest image concept
4. Review and publish
