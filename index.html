About This Project

Tafsir - Quran Journey is a personal project to make the Quran accessible, visual, and deeply understood. Each surah page includes interactive carousels with hand-drawn illustrations, detailed verse-by-verse explanations, and word-by-word Arabic breakdowns to help you learn Quranic Arabic naturally.

Whether you're a beginner exploring the Quran for the first time or someone who wants to go deeper into the meaning of the surahs you recite daily, this is for you.

Sources & Approach: All explanations are based on classical tafsir sources including Tafsir Ibn Kathir, Tafsir As-Sa'di, and Tafsir Al-Qurtubi. Hadith references are from authentic collections (Sahih Al-Bukhari, Sahih Muslim, etc.). This is a learning aid, not a scholarly work. Always verify with qualified scholars for detailed rulings and interpretations.
