Doodle Style Proof of Concept

Al-Fatiha carousel using Rough.js hand-drawn doodles

1
بِسْمِ اللَّهِ الرَّحْمَٰنِ الرَّحِيمِ
Bismi-llahi r-rahmani r-rahim
Every beautiful thing starts by remembering Allah. When you say "Bismillah", you open a door of light and blessings.
2
الْحَمْدُ لِلَّهِ رَبِّ الْعَالَمِينَ
Al-hamdu li-llahi rabbi l-'alamin
All thanks and praise belong to Allah - the One who made the stars, the mountains, the oceans, and everything you see.
3
الرَّحْمَٰنِ الرَّحِيمِ
Ar-rahmani r-rahim
Allah's mercy is everywhere - like rain that feeds gardens, like warmth from the sun. He cares for everything, always.
4
مَالِكِ يَوْمِ الدِّينِ
Maliki yawmi d-din
There will be a big day when Allah judges everyone fairly. Every good deed and every bad deed will be weighed on a scale.
5
إِيَّاكَ نَعْبُدُ وَإِيَّاكَ نَسْتَعِينُ
Iyyaka na'budu wa-iyyaka nasta'in
Only You we worship, only You we ask for help. Like a compass always pointing one direction - our hearts point to Allah alone.
6
اهْدِنَا الصِّرَاطَ الْمُسْتَقِيمَ
Ihdina s-sirata l-mustaqim
We ask Allah: "Show us the straight path!" - a clear, bright road through life, guided by His light.
7
صِرَاطَ الَّذِينَ أَنْعَمْتَ عَلَيْهِمْ غَيْرِ الْمَغْضُوبِ عَلَيْهِمْ وَلَا الضَّالِّينَ
Sirata l-ladhina an'amta 'alayhim ghayri l-maghdubi 'alayhim wa-la d-dallin
The path of those You blessed - not those who earned anger, not those who got lost. Keep us on the right road, always.

About this PoC

This uses Rough.js to draw all illustrations with a hand-drawn, doodle aesthetic. Every line, shape, and element looks like it was sketched on a whiteboard or notebook.

Key features: wobbly lines, hachure fills, hand-drawn circles, notebook dot grid background, handwriting font labels. No external images needed - everything is drawn live in the browser via Canvas.
